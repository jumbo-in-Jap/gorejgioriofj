How to use?

  • 1. Please allow to use mic/camera on your browser.
  • 2. Please reload this browser.
  • 3. Please say on your mic, then you can hear your voice on your speaker/headset.
  • ■ You Hear your voice
    **Success! Please waiting for your lesson.**
  • ■ You don't hear your voice
    **Failed, Please check below.**

  • If you change your device setting, please reload your broser.

If you cannot hear your voice,

  • Please check your device(mic/camera/speaker/pc...) is connected.
  • Please check your pc setting, enable to use your mic/camera/speaker.
  • Please close you application that is need to use mic/camera/speaker. (ex. Skype/Appear.in)
  • Please try to reload/restart browser.
